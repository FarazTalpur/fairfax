In emergencies, time is of the essence. Our team in Fairfax, VA is always on standby, ready to respond to your leak detection needs with immediacy and efficiency. We ensure that your call is answered promptly and our experts are dispatched to your location without delay.

Advanced Detection Methods

We utilize state-of-the-art technology to accurately pinpoint the source of leaks without causing unnecessary damage to your property. Our non-invasive techniques include:

  • Infrared Thermography: Detects hidden moisture and temperature variations.
  • Acoustic Leak Detection: Identifies leaks by listening for water escaping pipes.
  • Tracer Gas Detection: Uses harmless gas to trace hard-to-find leaks.

Experienced Technicians

Our team comprises highly trained and experienced leak detection specialists. With years of experience under their belts, they can handle any leak detection challenge, ensuring your property is safeguarded against water damage.
